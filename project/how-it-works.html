The process

Five steps, in order.

  • 01 · DiscoveryDiscovery call. Understand fit. A short conversation to see whether there is real operational pain worth diagnosing.
  • 02 · DiagnoseOperational diagnostic. Map the current workflow, enquiries, quotes, jobs, customers, admin, and where it breaks.
  • 03 · RecommendWorkflow recommendation. Identify the highest-value Phase 1, the fix that returns the most, fastest.
  • 04 · BuildBuild. We create the system around how the business actually operates. Weekly updates. You approve at each milestone.
  • 05 · EmbedEmbed. We stay involved to support, optimise and improve the system as the business grows.
