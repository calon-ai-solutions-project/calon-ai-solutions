Fire, Security & Electrical · Volt Secure · South Wales · 10 engineersTier 1 · Delivered

The problem

Enquiries, quotes and job information lived across phones, inboxes and spreadsheets. Timesheets were inflating against what the van GPS showed, discovered on payday Friday, under pressure. The founder was the operating system: the full picture lived in his head.

What Calon built

An operational platform: a central company dashboard, an engineer mobile app, a quote workflow, RAM Tracking GPS cross-reference for timesheet verification, a discrepancy dashboard and Xero export. One place to see enquiries, quotes, jobs and engineer activity, instead of asking around.

The result

  • Timesheet overpayment caught in real time
  • Payroll: hours of admin → minutes
  • Engineers self-corrected once the system was live
  • Quotes tracked from the moment they're sent
  • Company data centralised, one source of truth
  • Founder no longer the single point of visibility

"We were paying for hours that were never worked. The platform caught it in real time, and now the whole operation runs from one dashboard."

Hospitality · The Cwtch · CardiffTier 1 · Delivered

The problem

Enquiries arrived at all hours with no separation between a casual enquiry, a held date and a confirmed booking. Deposits weren't chased. Slow responses lost bookings and drew negative reviews.

What Calon built

An instant-response system in the venue's voice (24/7), three-stage booking logic (Enquiry / Held / Confirmed), automatic deposit chasing, a booking-priority dashboard showing competing enquiries side by side, and a post-event review trigger.

The result

  • 3–5 bookings recovered per month
  • Deposit chasing: 0% → 100% automatic
  • Held dates without deposits auto-released
  • System live in 5 weeks

"No customer gets ignored. Ever. The system answers in seconds regardless of what we're doing."

Antonio, Owner, The Cwtch
