01  |  Tier 1 · Delivered

Stopped timesheet leakage and cut payroll checking from hours to minutes.

Security & Electrical · Volt Secure · 10 engineers · South Wales Tier 1 · Delivered

Overview

Volt Secure was losing money through timesheet inflation, but the issue only became visible on payday Friday, when it was already difficult to challenge. Calon built a RAM Tracking connected timesheet verification system that compares engineer timesheets against van GPS and flags discrepancies before payroll is approved.

What we built

RAM Tracking integration, GPS cross-reference engine, discrepancy dashboard, one-click approve / query flow, Xero payroll export, audit trail, and mobile engineer timesheets.

Results

  • £20,800/year overpayment identified
  • £15,200 net year-one benefit
  • Payroll checking: 3–4 hours to under 10 minutes
  • Payback in under 5 months
  • Engineers self-corrected once the system went live

"The data vs the timesheet. There's no argument to have anymore."

Cai, Director, Volt Secure

Slow manual quoting became a fast, tracked proposal system.

Security & Electrical · Volt Secure · 10 engineers · South Wales Tier 2 · In Build

Volt Secure's manual quoting process was taking around 24 hours per proposal. 03  |  Tier 1 · Delivered

Stopped losing bookings from slow replies and unclear booking stages.

Hospitality · The Cwtch · Cardiff · built in 5 weeks Tier 1 · Delivered

Overview

The Cwtch was receiving enquiries during evenings, weekends and bank holidays, but there was no clear separation between casual enquiries, held dates and confirmed bookings. Deposits were not always chased, high-value bookings could be blocked by lower-value holds, and unanswered enquiries were creating reputation risk.

What we built

Instant 24/7 enquiry response system, three-stage booking logic (Enquiry / Held / Confirmed), deposit tracking, automatic 48-hour and 5-day chasing, booking priority dashboard, pipeline view, automated reminders, and a Google review trigger.

Results

  • 3 to 5 bookings recovered per month
  • Estimated £2,400 to £4,000/month recovered
  • System built in 5 weeks
  • Better booking visibility and fewer lost enquiries

"Every enquiry now gets an instant reply. We stopped losing bookings to slow responses. The system paid for itself in 6 weeks."

Antonio, Owner, The Cwtch
