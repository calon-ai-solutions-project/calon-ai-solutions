01  |  The Problem

The leak is rarely obvious. That's why it keeps costing you.

In most growing service businesses, the problem isn't effort, your team is already busy. The problem is that work moves through too many places: enquiries in inboxes, quotes in spreadsheets, job notes in WhatsApp, reminders in someone's head, customer history buried inside Xero. That's where time, margin and opportunities disappear.

01

Leads go cold

A website enquiry comes in. Nobody replies quickly enough. The customer moves on.

02

Quotes sit unchased

The quote was sent, but no one can see whether it was followed up.

03

Jobs move without visibility

The office, customer and engineer aren't always looking at the same information.

04

Admin repeats itself

The same customer details are entered into multiple systems, portals and spreadsheets.

05

The founder carries the process

If the founder is away, nobody has the full picture. Growth starts breaking things.

You cannot fix operational leakage if you cannot see where it's happening.
